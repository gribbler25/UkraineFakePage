With just $25/ month or more you are a cooperative member and can register yourself with the links provided for one or more areas of support needed in your own coutry for Ukrainian children and their families fleeing Russian aggression:

Housing
Offering temporary room/board for children and their families transitioning to your country/state/city
Food
Monetary support and/ or companion shopping and transportation support for new Ukrainian families in your state/town
Childcare
Offering engaging and loving care to young Ukrainian children while parents shop or have other responsibilities to manage in your state/town(background checks required)
Clothing
Make direct donations locally or organize a children's clothing drive in your area to add to the UCRC's international clothing resources(shipping costs covered)
